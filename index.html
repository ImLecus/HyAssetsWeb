HyAssets by Powerbyte7

HyAssets1.1

A collection of Hytale 3D models by Powerbyte7

Multiple file format

Every creature is avaliable in 4 different formats and they have some textures to alternate with. Actually it brings some prefabricated characters, as the kweebecs and zombies.

fully rigged models

Models are completely rigged and ready to animate. Each extremity of their body is divided to make the rigging process practically null for you!

Item models

Additionally, it has some JSON models for some items and a big variety of block textures. That brings the possibility to create your own landscapes.
